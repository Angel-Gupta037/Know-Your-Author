The Story

Every great author
has a best first book.

Most people never find it.

I built Know Your Author because I kept running into the same problem — I'd hear about an author, want to read them, search online, and get hit with a list of their most famous books. But famous doesn't mean first.

Dostoevsky's most celebrated work isn't where you should start. Neither is Kafka's. Neither is Orwell's. The internet is full of "best books" lists. Nobody tells you where to actually begin.

There's something quietly frustrating about wanting to enter an author's world and not knowing which door to knock on. You pick the wrong book, it's too dense, too abstract, too heavy for a first encounter — and you give up on an author you might have loved.

"Know Your Author is my answer to that. A simple, honest guide to entering an author's world — starting from the right door."

— The idea behind KYA

Every reading path here is personally curated. Not algorithmic. Not a bestseller list. Just a genuine recommendation of where to begin, and why — written for someone who has never opened that author's work before.

This is not Goodreads. This is not a review site. This is just one reader telling another: start here, trust me.

Know Your Author started as a small project — a way to solve a problem I kept having. If it helps even one person discover an author they end up loving, it's done its job.

Start somewhere. Start right.

— Angel
